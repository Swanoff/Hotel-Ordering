Rooms

The Sea Hotel has 71 centrally air-conditioned, full service rooms. It offers a wide variety and type of rooms.

The executive rooms come with a choice of a king size bed or twin beds. Coffee kettles, hair dryers, wooden floors and a cozier room ambience are a part of all the executive rooms. Bath tubs and a great line up of guest amenities are also standard. Complimentary breakfast and usage of our state of the art health club is also included in the tariff.
